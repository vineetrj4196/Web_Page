Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played worldwide. It features two teams of 11 players each, with one team batting and the other fielding. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and defending wickets. Cricket is known for its diverse formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) games, each with unique rules and strategies

Carrom

Carrom is a tabletop game that originated in South Asia, particularly popular in India. Played by two or four players, it involves flicking small discs with a striker into pockets at the corners of a square board. The game combines precision, strategy, and aiming skills, making it a beloved family and social pastime. Carrom tournaments also exist, showcasing top-level competition.
